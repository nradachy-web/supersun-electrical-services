Electrical Repair FAQ

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency service. For urgent issues like burning smells, sparking outlets, or complete power loss, call (737) 215-4657 immediately. We prioritize safety-critical calls and respond as fast as possible.

Why do my lights flicker when I turn on an appliance?

This usually means the appliance is on a circuit that can't handle the load, or there's a loose connection somewhere in the circuit. It could also indicate your panel needs upgrading. We'll diagnose the exact cause during our consultation.

Is a tripping breaker dangerous?

A breaker tripping occasionally is the safety mechanism working as designed. But if it trips repeatedly, that indicates an underlying issue — overloaded circuit, short circuit, or ground fault — that needs professional diagnosis. Don't just keep resetting it.

Do you repair aluminum wiring?

Yes. Aluminum wiring (common in 1960s-70s homes) requires special attention due to fire risk at connections. We can install COPALUM or AlumiConn connectors, or recommend a full rewire depending on your situation.

Don't Ignore Electrical Problems.

Small issues become dangerous hazards. Get a proper diagnosis from a Master Electrician — before it becomes an emergency.
