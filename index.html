Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an EV charger installation cost?

Every home is different. Cost depends on your panel capacity, distance to the garage, and whether a subpanel or upgrades are needed. We provide a thorough paid consultation to evaluate your system and give you an accurate, no-surprise quote.

Why do you charge for consultations instead of giving free estimates?

Because "free estimates" are usually guesses. Our paid consultation means a Master Electrician evaluates your entire electrical system — not just the surface issue. You get a professional diagnosis and transparent pricing, so there are no surprises on install day.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Supersun holds Texas Electrical Contractors License #39536 via TDLR. We're also certified by Tesla, SPAN, ChargePoint, and Eaton — meaning we're trained and authorized on the industry's best equipment.
