Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an EV charger installation cost?

Every home is different — cost depends on your panel capacity, distance to the garage, and whether upgrades are needed. We provide a thorough paid consultation to evaluate your system and give you an accurate, no-surprise quote. No ballpark guesses.

Why do you charge for consultations?

Because "free estimates" are usually guesses that lead to surprise bills. Our paid consultation means a Master Electrician evaluates your entire electrical system — not just the surface issue. You get a professional diagnosis and transparent pricing. The consultation fee is applied toward your project if you move forward.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Supersun holds Texas Electrical Contractors License #39536 via TDLR. We're also certified by Tesla, SPAN, ChargePoint, and Eaton — meaning we're factory-trained and authorized on the industry's best equipment. Fully insured and background checked.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We've partnered with Synchrony Bank to offer flexible financing options. Upgrade your home's electrical system without the full upfront cost — from panel upgrades to EV chargers.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater North Austin area including Round Rock, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Cedar Park, Leander, Liberty Hill, Hutto, Jarrell, Wells Branch, Manor, Bee Cave, Lakeway, Lago Vista, Jonestown, Steiner Ranch, West Lake Hills, and more.

Do you handle emergencies?

Absolutely — we offer 24/7 emergency electrical services. Call us at (737) 215-4657 any time, day or night. Power outages, sparking outlets, or burning smells — we're there when it matters most.
