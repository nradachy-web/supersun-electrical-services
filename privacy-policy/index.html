If you opt in to SMS communications, we may send appointment reminders and service updates via text message. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying "STOP" to any message.

We use cookies for website personalization, traffic analysis, preference storage, and performance tracking. By continuing to use our website, you consent to cookie usage.

Under CCPA and GDPR (where applicable), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Requests will be responded to within 30 days. Contact us at hello@supersunelectric.com to exercise these rights.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.
