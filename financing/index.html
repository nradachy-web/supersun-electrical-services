Financing FAQ

What financing terms are available?

Synchrony Bank offers multiple financing options with various term lengths and promotional rates. Specific terms and rates depend on the approved credit amount and selected plan. We'll walk you through all available options during your consultation.

Is there a minimum project size for financing?

Financing is available for most project sizes. Whether it's a single EV charger installation or a full panel upgrade with smart home integration, we can help you find a payment plan that works.

Does applying affect my credit score?

The initial application may involve a credit inquiry. For specific details on how the application process works, we recommend discussing directly with Synchrony Bank during the application.

Can I combine financing with the warranty?

Absolutely. All financed projects receive the same lifetime labor warranty on original installation as any other Supersun project. Financing doesn't change our commitment to quality.
