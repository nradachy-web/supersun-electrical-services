Smart Home FAQ

What is a SPAN panel?

SPAN is a smart electrical panel that replaces your traditional breaker box. It gives you circuit-level control from your phone — monitor energy usage, turn circuits on/off remotely, prioritize power during outages, and integrate with solar and battery systems. Think of it as the brain of your home's electrical system.

Do I need to rewire my home for smart features?

Usually not. Most smart switches and outlets are designed to replace existing ones using your current wiring. SPAN panels replace your existing panel. For new construction or major renovations, we can add structured wiring for a more comprehensive smart home setup.

Will smart home features work with my voice assistant?

Yes. The products we install are compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, and other major platforms. We'll help you configure everything so it works seamlessly with your preferred ecosystem.

Is a SPAN panel worth the investment?

If you have solar, a battery backup, an EV, or just want granular control over your energy usage — absolutely. SPAN pays for itself through energy optimization and is essential for modern homes. It's especially valuable during Texas storms when power prioritization matters most.
