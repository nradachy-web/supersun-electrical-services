Lifetime Labor Warranty

We stand behind every wire, every connection, and every install. Our lifetime labor warranty on original installations means your investment is protected for as long as you own your home.

Our Promise

What's Covered

Our warranty covers all installation-related issues from the original work performed by Supersun Electrical Services.

✅

Workmanship Defects

Any defects in workmanship from the original installation are covered. If a connection fails, a circuit malfunctions, or anything we installed isn't performing correctly — we fix it at no charge.

✅

Improper Connections

If any connection made during the original installation is found to be improper or insecure, we will return and correct it immediately — no questions asked.

✅

Code Violations from Installation

If any part of our original installation is found to not meet code requirements, we will bring it up to code at our expense. We build to code the first time — but if something is missed, we make it right.

✅

Manufacturer Product Warranties

We honor all manufacturer product warranties on equipment we install. If your Tesla Wall Connector, SPAN panel, or any installed product fails under the manufacturer's warranty, we coordinate the replacement.

Warranty Duration

Our lifetime labor warranty is valid for the lifetime of the current owner's residence at their current property. This warranty covers the original installation only and does not transfer to new property owners.

What's Not Covered

To keep things transparent, here's what falls outside our warranty coverage.

❌

Unrelated Electrical Problems

Electrical issues not related to the original installation — problems in different areas of your home, pre-existing conditions, or issues caused by other work.

❌

New Electrical Needs

Additional circuits, outlets, upgrades, or new electrical projects that go beyond the scope of the original installation.

❌

Normal Wear & Tear

Components that degrade over time through normal use, weather-related damage (except surge protection claims), or user-caused issues.

❌

Third-Party Modifications

Any modifications or repairs made to our original installation by unauthorized third parties will void the warranty for the affected work.

Warranty FAQ

Does the warranty transfer if I sell my home?

No. The lifetime labor warranty is valid for the current owner at their current property only. It does not transfer to new homeowners.

What voids the warranty?

The warranty is voided by: unauthorized modifications to the warranted work, third-party repairs to the original installation, delayed issue reporting (unreasonable delay after discovering a problem), or misuse of installed systems.

Does this cover my EV charger if it stops working?

If the charger failure is due to our installation (wiring, connections, circuit), yes — that's covered. If the charger unit itself fails, that falls under the manufacturer's product warranty, which we help coordinate.

How quickly do you respond to warranty claims?

We treat warranty claims with the same urgency as new work. Most claims are inspected within 48 hours. Safety-critical issues are prioritized immediately.
